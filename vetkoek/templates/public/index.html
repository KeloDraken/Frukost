A space for friends

Foxstraat is a retro social network focused on privacy and customisability.
It's a friendly place to have fun, meet friends, and be creative.

Fully customisable

Foxstraat allows you to add custom HTML and CSS code to your profile to give you all the freedom you need to make your profile truly your your Straat on the web!

Privacy friendly

Foxstraat has no algorithms, no tracking, and no personalised ads - feeds on Foxstraat are chronological and there is no suggested content begging for your attention. You decide what you want to share and what content you'd like to view.
